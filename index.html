About

Established in 1996 and situated between Toronto and Montreal in the stunning Land O' Lakes Region, T.R. MacMunn & Son's is a trusted name in dimensional signs.

First impressions last a lifetime. Our mission is to help you put your best foot forward. Whether it's carved cedar guiding friends to the cottage, a vintage design that fills a room with character, or a mural that inspires a sense of history, we have the right sign for you.
